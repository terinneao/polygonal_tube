info
Design Insight: Polygonal tubes are highly efficient structural members. Due to their multiple axes of symmetry, their moment of inertia is isotropic (identical in both horizontal and vertical bending plane), giving uniform bending resistance regardless of orientation. However, the outermost fiber distance and combined yielding capacity varies between flat-to-flat and corner-to-corner bending.
